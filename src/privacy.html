Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data AdminWorks collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have — in plain language, fully aligned with EU and EEA privacy law.

1About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data processed by AdminWorks Ltd. through our website (adminworks.eu), our client portal, and in the course of providing outsourced administrative services.

It has been written in compliance with:

  • Regulation (EU) 2016/679 — General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Romanian Law No. 190/2018 — national GDPR implementing measures
  • ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC as amended — electronic communications privacy
  • EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) — transparency obligations for AI-assisted processing
  • EDPB Guidelines — including updated guidelines on transparency (04/2023) and consent (05/2020)

📌 If you are a client business whose employee or customer data AdminWorks processes on your behalf, that processing is governed by a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA) rather than this policy.

4How We Collect Your Data

We collect personal data through the following means:

  • Direct interactions — when you fill in a contact form, book a call, register for the client portal, send us an email, or provide instructions for task delivery
  • Automated technologies — when you visit our website, we collect technical and usage data through server logs and privacy-first analytics (Plausible). We do not use invasive tracking pixels or fingerprinting
  • Third parties — we may receive basic company and contact information from business directories or referral partners, always subject to their own privacy obligations

6Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Sub-processors — trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf under binding DPAs (see our sub-processor list)
  • Legal authorities — where required by law, court order, or regulatory obligation in Romania or the EU
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections and advance notice to affected individuals
  • Professional advisors — lawyers, auditors, and insurers bound by professional confidentiality obligations

All third parties are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with GDPR.

7Marketing Communications

We may send marketing communications about our services in the following circumstances:

  • Existing clients — we may contact you about similar or complementary services to those you have already purchased, under the soft opt-in rule (Recital 47 GDPR, ePrivacy Directive Art. 13(2)). You can opt out at any time.
  • Prospects who consent — where you have explicitly opted in via a form or checkbox, we will send you relevant updates, case studies, and service information.

Every marketing email includes a clear and functional unsubscribe link. Opt-out requests are processed within 5 business days. We do not use purchased contact lists.

⚠️ Withdrawing marketing consent does not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before withdrawal, nor does it affect processing carried out under other legal bases such as contract performance.

8Profiling & Automated Decision-Making

AdminWorks may use basic profiling to personalise service communications — for example, identifying which services a client uses to send relevant updates. This profiling has no legal or significant effect on individuals.

We do not use fully automated decision-making (within the meaning of Article 22 GDPR) in our service delivery. Where AI-assisted tools are used to support task processing (e.g. drafting, summarising, or categorising content), a human team member always reviews and takes responsibility for the output, in compliance with the EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689).

9Children's Privacy

Our services are directed exclusively at businesses and are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us at privacy@adminworks.eu and we will delete it promptly.

10Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, accidental loss, destruction, or disclosure. These include:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for data at rest
  • Role-based access controls — team members only access data necessary for their tasks
  • Multi-factor authentication on all internal systems
  • Regular security training for all staff
  • Annual penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Signed NDAs and confidentiality agreements for all personnel

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. In the event of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you in accordance with our obligations under Article 34 GDPR. See our GDPR page for full breach procedure details.

11Your Rights

Under GDPR (Articles 15–22) and Romanian Law 190/2018, you have the right to:

  • Access — obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected
  • Erasure — request deletion where there is no longer a lawful basis to hold it
  • Restriction — ask us to pause processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests, including profiling and direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent — at any time where processing is consent-based
  • Lodge a complaint — with the ANSPDCP or your local EU supervisory authority

To exercise any right, email privacy@adminworks.eu with your request and proof of identity. We respond within 30 days, free of charge. Complex requests may be extended by a further 60 days with notice.

📋 You may lodge a complaint with the ANSPDCP at www.dataprotection.ro, or with the supervisory authority in your EU country of residence or workplace.

Where changes are material, we will notify active clients by email at least 14 days before the updated policy takes effect. Continued use of our services after that date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
